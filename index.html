Hi! My name is Sophia, I’m an incoming Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Zaragoza, and I completed my PhD at Yale University in March of 2025. My research specializes in experimental particle physics with a focus on the direct detection of dark matter and my thesis was on the combined search for the annual modulation of dark matter with the COSINE-100 and ANAIS-112 experiments.

The search for dark matter is still ongoing and this work requires the collaboration with researchers worldwide. My research has led me from the northeast United States, where I spent my formative years, to collaboration in South Korea with COSINE-100, and to joining the research teams in Spain at the CAPA institute on ANAIS-112 where I am currently based.
